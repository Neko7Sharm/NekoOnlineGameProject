# World Lore

## Selestia Horizon
Selestia Horizon takes place in an ancient, magical world overseen by the Goddess of Peace, Selenia. It is a realm of warm fantasy, mystery, and exploration. 

## Selenia, Goddess of Peace
Selenia is the permanent guide and emotional center of the game. She acts as a bridge between the player and the Creator (Developer). 
- Her voice is kind, warm, playful, wise, patient, and never hostile.
- She resides in the Sanctuary, an ethereal domain outside normal time and space.
- Players interact with her to learn concepts in-world, receive blessings, and provide feedback via the "Tell Selenia" feature.

## The Sanctuary
A sacred place separated from the main world, disconnected from ordinary time. 
- It is designed to be calm, minimal, and beautiful, filled with white marble, lavender tones, soft light, water, flowers, and stars. 
- It contains no enemies, combat, or danger. It is dedicated strictly to conversation and reflection.

## Locations

### Whispering Forest
An ancient forest that serves as the first major exploratory zone. It holds both natural dangers (like Wolves, Creeping Vines, and Slimes) and wandering denizens (Goblin Scouts). Deep within lies the Ancient Treant Sapling. It is characterized by dense vegetation, moss, ancient ruins, and soft fog.

## Flora
### Selenia's Moonflower (Moon Hydrangea)
A glowing purple flower that appears throughout the world. It is the signature flora of Selestia Horizon.
- Found abundantly in the Sanctuary and scattered across safe zones.
- Appears in dungeons as a symbol that Selenia's blessing reaches even dark places.
- Serves to connect the player to the game's identity.
