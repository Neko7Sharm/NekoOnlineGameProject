# Audio Bible

## Music Direction
Music in Selestia Horizon should always support exploration, emotion, and the peaceful atmosphere of the world. It should never feel intrusive or overwhelming.

## Primary Instruments
- Felt Piano
- Harp
- Celesta
- Soft Strings
- Choir Pads

## Avoid
- Electronic
- Dubstep
- Rock
- Heavy percussion

## Specific Themes
- **Sanctuary Theme**: Should always prioritize an emotional, gentle piano melody to reinforce the feeling of safety and home.
- **Village Theme**: Warm, acoustic, and welcoming.
- **Forest Theme**: Mysterious, soft, with ambient natural sounds.
- **Combat Theme**: Should be energetic but maintain a fantasy, orchestral/acoustic feel without turning into heavy metal or dubstep.
