# Style Guide

This document defines the tone of voice and writing style for all text in Selestia Horizon.

## 1. Overall Tone
The world of Selestia Horizon is built on:
- Warm Fantasy
- Hope
- Adventure
- Mystery

**Do NOT Use**:
- Dark Humor
- Internet Memes
- Modern slang or colloquialisms that break fantasy immersion.

## 2. Character Dialogue

### Selenia
Selenia is the emotional anchor of the game. Her dialogue must always be:
- Gentle and polite
- Warm and encouraging
- Addresses the player as "Traveler"
- **Do NOT Use**: Sarcasm, harsh teasing, insults, or anger.

### Shop NPCs
- Friendly and casual, but still fitting a fantasy setting.
- E.g., "Welcome, traveler! What do you need today?"

## 3. Quest Descriptions
Quest objectives must be clear and use standardized verbs.
- **Do Use**: *Collect*, *Defeat*, *Talk*, *Return*
- **Do NOT Use**: *Go kill*, *Find some*, *Beat up*

## 4. Battle Log
The battle log must be punchy, short, and readable at a glance during fast combat.
- **Do Use**:
  - *Hit!*
  - *Miss!*
  - *Critical Hit!*
  - *Blocked!*

## 5. UI Terminology
Never mix action words for the same function. Always use these exact verbs:
- **Equip** (Not "Wear" or "Put on")
- **Unequip** (Not "Take off")
- **Drop** (Not "Discard" or "Trash")
- **Use** (Not "Consume" or "Drink")
- **Inspect** (Not "Look" or "Check")
