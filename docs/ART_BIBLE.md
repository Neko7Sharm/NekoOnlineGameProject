# 27. Art Bible & Design Theme

Selestia Horizon follows a consistent visual identity inspired by classic fantasy JRPGs.

The world should always feel magical, warm, peaceful, and timeless rather than realistic or modern.

Every asset should look like it belongs to the same world.

---

## Overall Art Style

**Primary Style**
- Fantasy JRPG
- Pixel Art
- Painterly Concept Art
- Soft Fantasy Illustration

The game combines:
- Pixel art for gameplay
- Painterly illustrations for portraits and character sheets
- Soft fantasy UI

**Avoid**:
- Photorealism
- Modern anime aesthetics

---

## Art Keywords

Always prioritize these keywords when creating assets:
- Warm Fantasy, Moonlight, Cozy, Peaceful, Sacred, Hope, Guidance
- Stars, Moon, Ancient Forest, White Marble, Lavender, Purple
- Soft Glow, Glowing Flowers
- Pixel Art, Fantasy JRPG, Painterly, Elegant, Timeless

---

## Character Design

Characters should look elegant rather than exaggerated.

**Style**:
- Fantasy JRPG
- Soft painterly illustration
- Gentle expressions
- Elegant fantasy clothing
- Clear silhouette

**Avoid**:
- Modern fashion, School uniforms, Sci-fi armor, Cyberpunk
- Oversized accessories, Excessive fanservice

Characters should feel like they belong to the world of Selestia.

### Character Proportions

**Portraits**:
- Semi-anime proportions
- Natural anatomy
- Soft facial features
- Expressive eyes
- Elegant poses

**Sprites**:
- Readable silhouette
- Simplified details
- Consistent proportions
- Pixel-art friendly

---

## Color Palette

**Primary**: Moon White, Lavender, Soft Purple, Royal Violet, Midnight Blue
**Secondary**: Forest Green, Warm Brown, Stone Gray, Gold
**Accent**: Moonlight Blue, Glowing Violet, Soft Cyan

*Avoid highly saturated colors.*

---

## Selenia Design Rules

Selenia must always maintain the same identity.

**Appearance**:
- Long lavender hair
- Purple eyes
- Gentle smile
- Moon Hydrangea hair ornament
- White and lavender dress
- Gold decorations
- Elegant Star Staff

**Personality**:
- Kind, Patient, Warm, Gentle, Encouraging, Hopeful

**Never portray Selenia as**:
- Aggressive, Evil, Cold, Arrogant, Comedic mascot

---

## Moon Hydrangea

**Official Name**: Selenia's Moonflower
**Common Name**: Moonflower
**Species**: Moon Hydrangea

**Visual Rules**:
- Purple petals
- Soft moonlight glow
- Elegant
- Sacred
- Never oversized
- Always appears healthy

**Symbolizes**: Hope, Protection, Safe journeys, Selenia's blessing

---

## Environment Design

The world should always feel peaceful. Even dangerous areas should feel mysterious instead of horrific. Avoid horror aesthetics.

### Theme Sets (Biomes)

**🌙 Selestia Village Set**
- Stone roads (multiple shades, cracks, moss, grass in cracks)
- Wooden houses (warm tones, varying roofs, consistent perspective/scale)
- Moonflowers, small trees, warm lanterns, wooden fences, benches, crates, barrels
- Buildings should have different identities (e.g., Inn: moon sign, warm windows, flower pots; Blacksmith: chimney, anvil, coals; Shop: signboard, crates).

**🏛 Sanctuary Set**
- White marble
- Clear calm water, floating petals
- Moon Hydrangeas, purple runes
- Starlight and moonlight, soft lighting
- No danger, no enemies. Should feel like home.

**🌲 Whispering Forest Set**
- Dense vegetation, mossy rocks
- Large trees, thick roots
- Soft fog, moonlight penetrating canopy
- Glowing wild Moonflowers
- Hidden paths and ancient ruins

**⚔️ Dungeon Set**
- Should feel Ancient, Magical, Forgotten.
- **Never**: Bloody, Gory, Horror, Dark fantasy.
- Players should always feel curiosity instead of fear.

### Blending and Edges
Avoid sharp cuts between structures and the ground. Use shadows under eaves, bushes, stones, and flowers to blend edges naturally into the terrain.

---

## Lighting
**Preferred**: Moonlight, Lanterns, Glowing flowers, Soft bloom, Magical particles.
**Avoid**: Harsh sunlight, Neon, Heavy contrast, Modern lighting.

---

## Visual Effects
Combat effects should be: Fast, Readable, Bright, Impactful.
Inspired by classic JRPGs and MapleStory.
**Preferred colors**: White, Light Blue, Cyan, Purple.
Weapon effects should disappear quickly after impact.

---

## AI Asset Generation Rules

Whenever generating assets using AI:
- Maintain consistent proportions.
- Follow the established color palette.
- Keep every asset within the same fantasy world.
- Prioritize readability over excessive detail.
- All illustrations should resemble official RPG artwork rather than anime fanart.
- Pixel assets should be based on the same concept art.

## Negative Design Rules
**Do NOT generate**:
Sci-Fi, Cyberpunk, Modern cities, Modern clothing, Realistic humans, Photorealism, VTuber style, Idol style, Gothic horror, Zombies, Blood, Gore, Neon colors, Oversized weapons, Excessive particle effects.

Every design should reinforce the identity of Selestia Horizon as a peaceful fantasy RPG guided by the Moon Goddess Selenia.
