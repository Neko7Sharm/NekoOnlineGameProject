# Prompt Guide

This document stores the standardized AI prompts used to generate assets for Selestia Horizon. Using these prompts ensures that every generated asset matches the game's art direction and identity.

## 1. Character Portrait
**Prompt**: 
> "A beautiful, soft painterly fantasy JRPG character portrait of [CHARACTER DESCRIPTION]. Elegant fantasy clothing. Gentle expression. Warm moonlight lighting, glowing particles, soft background. Masterpiece, official RPG concept art, highly detailed, expressive eyes. Do not use modern clothing or sci-fi elements."

## 2. NPC Sprite / Pixel Art
**Prompt**: 
> "16-bit RPG pixel art sprite of [NPC DESCRIPTION]. Clear silhouette, warm colors, facing forward/angled, transparent background style. Fantasy JRPG aesthetic, simple and readable design without excessive visual noise."

## 3. Monster
**Prompt**: 
> "A fantasy JRPG enemy monster: [MONSTER DESCRIPTION]. Painterly illustration style. Threatening but not horrific or gory. Forest environment lighting, magical elements. Official RPG concept art."

## 4. Building / Town Asset
**Prompt**: 
> "Isometric 2D fantasy RPG building: [BUILDING TYPE, e.g., Wooden Inn]. Warm lighting, stone base, wooden walls, fantasy tile roof. Surrounded by small glowing purple Moonflowers and grass. Painterly concept art style, cozy, welcoming, no modern elements."

## 5. Music
**Prompt**: 
> "[MOOD, e.g., Peaceful, Mysterious] fantasy RPG background music. Instruments: Felt piano, harp, soft strings, choir pad. No percussion, no electronic sounds, no heavy bass. Evokes a sense of warmth, safety, and moonlight."
